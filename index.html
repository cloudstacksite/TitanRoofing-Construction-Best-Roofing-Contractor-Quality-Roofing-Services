Comprehensive Roofing Solutions for Your Business

At Titan Roofing, we specialize in a variety of commercial roofing systems. Whether you need a new roof installed or a replacement for an old, deteriorating roof, we have the expertise to guide you through the process.

Below are some of the most popular commercial roofing options we offer:

1. TPO Roofing (Thermoplastic Olefin)

TPO roofing is a highly popular choice for commercial buildings due to its combination of energy efficiency, durability, and cost-effectiveness. Thermoplastic Olefin roofs reflect sunlight, which helps to lower cooling costs, and are resistant to UV rays, making them an excellent option for Arizona’s hot climate.

2. EPDM Roofing (Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer)

EPDM roofing is a rubber-like membrane that is known for its outstanding durability and resistance to environmental factors. EPDM roofs are perfect for flat or low-slope commercial buildings and are especially suited for Arizona’s weather, offering long-lasting protection against UV rays, rain, and extreme temperatures.

3. Metal Roofing

Metal roofing offers unmatched durability, especially for commercial properties in areas prone to extreme weather. Its ability to withstand high winds, hail, and even fire makes it a top choice for businesses looking for long-term roofing solutions. Metal roofs are also a cost-effective option, as they can often be repaired and coated to extend their lifespan, saving you money on full replacements.

4. Spray Polyurethane Foam (SPF)

SPF roofing is a highly versatile option offering superior insulation while providing a durable shield against the elements. SPF roofs are resistant to water, adaptable to various roof structures, and help increase energy efficiency in commercial buildings.

5. Elastomeric Coating

Elastomeric coatings are a smart option for commercial roofs, especially those looking for flexibility and resilience. This coating can be applied to metal and concrete roofs to create a waterproof barrier that stretches and contracts with the movements of the building..

6. Shingles

Shingle roofing, though traditionally associated with residential homes, is also a reliable option for commercial buildings. Asphalt shingles are particularly popular for their durability, affordability, and versatility.

7. Tile Roofing

Tile roofing, whether made from clay or concrete, is a durable and aesthetically pleasing roofing option for commercial properties. Known for its fire and insect resistance, tile roofing offers long-term protection against the elements.
